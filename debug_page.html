      Overview

      This Crop Protection Compendium channel has been designed to meet the need for improved dissemination of knowledge on crop pests and to support decision making in plant health. It has global scope and is updated regularly to include new scientific information including data from specialised organisations.

Por lo general, la informacin no lo identifica directamente, pero puede proporcionarle una experiencia web ms personalizada.
